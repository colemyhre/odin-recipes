Le Bowl

Description

Below is a recipe for Le Bowl. This is a recipe based on the famous "Le Bowl" salad from Zinque

There are some slight differences from the restaurant version, but this is a quick, delicious, healthy recipe that is great for a weeknight meal

Ingredients

  • Rotissere Chicken
  • Roma Tomatos
  • Comte Cheese
  • Arugula
  • Lemon
  • Brown Rice
  • Avocado
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sriracha (Optional)

Steps

  1. Break down rotissere chicken into small pieces, and heat up in a pan
  2. Dice tomatos and comte cheese
  3. Cook rice. We use microwaved frozen rice
  4. Place rice in a large bowl
  5. Lay down arugula on top of the rice
  6. Place warm chicken on top of the rice and arugula
  7. Add the cheese, tomatos, and avocado
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste
  9. Squeeze lemon over bowl, and add a light drizzle of olive oil
  10. (Optional) Add sriracha or any hot sauce of your choice
