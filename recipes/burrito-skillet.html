Burrito Skillet

Description

Below is a recipe for a burrito skillet. This has many of the same ingredients as a burrito, but is made in a one-pot style

This recipe can be modified in several different ways. For example, we have started using ground turkey instead of ground beef recently.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef or Turkey
  • Black Beans
  • Rice
  • Corn
  • Shredded Cheese Blend
  • Primal Kitchen Ranch
  • Salsa
  • Chips (on the side)
  • Avocado (optional)

Steps

  1. Brown the meat in a pan
  2. Once meat is cooked, add in cooked rice and mix to combine
  3. In a bowl, combine the beans, corn, salsa, and ranch
  4. Combine the bean/rice/corn mixture with the meat and rice in the pan
  5. Top with cheese, and bake in the oven at 400F for 25 minutes
  6. Top with avocado, and additional salsa if needed
  7. Serve with chips on the side
