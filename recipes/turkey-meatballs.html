Turkey Meatballs

Description

Below is a recipe for turkey meatballs. These can be used in a variety of other recipes, such as pasta, or meatball sandwiches

Ground beef can be substituted for ground turkey if you want!

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • White Onions
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Dice onion
  2. Finely chop the garlic
  3. Combine all ingredients together in a large mixing bowl until homogenous
  4. Shape mixture into small balls, about the size of a golf ball
  5. Place meat balls on a baking sheet
  6. Bake meat balls at 375F for 30 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
